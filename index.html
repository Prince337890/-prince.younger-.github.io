03 / Compliance & Anti-Fraud

Your Freight, Your Money — Protected

2026 brought a wave of freight fraud and a Supreme Court ruling that raised the stakes on who you do business with. We treat broker vetting as a hard requirement — because the fastest way to lose a paycheck is booking with the wrong middleman.

/01

Double-Brokering Defense

We screen every broker for the red flags of fraud — spoofed domains, suspicious urgency, last-minute swaps — so your load isn't re-brokered out from under you and left unpaid.

/02

Bond & Authority Checks

We verify the broker's $75k FMCSA bond is active and they're not on the suspended list — before your truck rolls. Booking with a suspended broker means you don't get paid. We don't take that risk.

/03

Airtight Proof Packages

Signed Rate Con, timestamped check-calls, signed BOL/POD, and lumper receipts on every load — the documentation that gets you paid and wins a claim if a broker defaults.

09 / Questions, Answered

The Honest FAQ

Straight answers — no fine print.

How is this different from a load board like DAT?+

A load board shows you freight to bid on — that's it. It won't tell you if the rate is profitable, whether the broker pays, or how to route to kill deadhead. Forward OS is the layer on top: source loads wherever you do now, then we price them against a rate floor, vet the broker, plan the triangle, and run the paperwork. The board finds the load; we make the load make money.

How is it different from a big TMS like McLeod or AscendTMS?+

Those are heavy record-keeping systems built for fleets with a back office — powerful, pricey, and a steep climb. Forward OS is dispatcher- and owner-operator-first: opinionated tools that actually coach the deal (rate floor, HOS legality, broker fraud-check, TriHaul routing), Guided Mode that teaches a new dispatcher, and a driver portal carriers like. Lighter, cheaper, focused on the decision — not just the filing.

Is my data private — can other dispatchers see my carriers?+

No. Every dispatcher gets a fully isolated workspace. You only ever see your own carriers, loads, and notes — separation is built into the foundation, not bolted on.

Do I get paid through the app?+

The app calculates your dispatch fee on every load and rolls it into a weekly total, so billing is exact. You collect from the carrier the way dispatchers do today (Zelle/ACH, or have the factoring company remit your fee). Automated split-pay is on the roadmap.

There's no ELD or live load-board feed yet — does that matter?+

Not for what this does today. Carriers self-report hours with one tap so every offer gets checked against their clock, and you source freight however you already do. ELD and load-board integrations are on the roadmap — the core dispatching value is here now.

I'm not techy — is it hard to use?+

If you can use a load board, you can use this. Guided Mode walks you through every screen, there's a first-login tour, and carriers onboard themselves with a link you send them.

What does it cost?+

It's free during the beta. Right now we want your feedback more than your money.
