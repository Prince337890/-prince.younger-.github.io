Compliance & Anti-Fraud

Your Freight, Your Money — Protected

2026 brought a wave of freight fraud and a Supreme Court ruling that raised the stakes on who you do business with. We treat broker vetting as a hard requirement — because the fastest way to lose a paycheck is booking with the wrong middleman.

Double-Brokering Defense

We screen every broker for the red flags of fraud — spoofed domains, suspicious urgency, last-minute swaps — so your load isn't re-brokered out from under you and left unpaid.

Bond & Authority Checks

We verify the broker's $75k FMCSA bond is active and they're not on the suspended list — before your truck rolls. Booking with a suspended broker means you don't get paid. We don't take that risk.

Airtight Proof Packages

Signed Rate Con, timestamped check-calls, signed BOL/POD, and lumper receipts on every load — the documentation that gets you paid and wins a claim if a broker defaults.
